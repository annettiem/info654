When I am out, and can afford it, I try to eat somewhere I haven’t tried yet or if I have eaten there, then I order something different. Unfortunately, shortly after moving here I discovered I don’t like pizza (sacrilegious I know). However, my friend Anais says that maybe I just haven’t tried good pizza so I am still open to trying different kinds.

I try to take a picture of everything I try but honestly, sometimes I’m too hungry to wait. For the times I did remember, I decided to chart everything on a tier list below. The tier is ranked from top, or ‘S’, being the best to bottom, or ‘F’, being the worst.
