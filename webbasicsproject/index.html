  • You really say "y'all"?
    • Yes :) And I am not sure I will ever stop (or want to)
  • What made you move all the way over here?
    • I moved for grad school but also I really wanted a change of pace.
  • What do you think of New York so far?
    • I love it here! I hope to spend as many years here as I can
